Nearby Attractions

Ujjain is one of India's most sacred cities with numerous spiritual and historical sites.

Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, this ancient Shiva temple is the most sacred site in Ujjain, just 2 km from the station.

2.5 km from property

Ram Ghat

Sacred ghat on the Shipra River where pilgrims take holy dips and witness spectacular evening aarti ceremonies.

3 km from property

Kal Bhairav Temple

Unique temple where liquor is offered to the fierce manifestation of Lord Shiva as Bhairava.

2.8 km from property

Sandipani Ashram

Ancient ashram where Lord Krishna is said to have studied along with Sudama and Balarama.

1.5 km from property

ISKCON Ujjain

Peaceful Krishna temple with beautiful architecture and devotional atmosphere.

3.2 km from property

Kaliadeh Palace

Historic palace on the banks of Shipra River with scenic views and interesting architecture.

5 km from property

Popular Excursions

Omkareshwar (2 hours)

Visit the sacred Jyotirlinga on an island in the Narmada River.

Maheshwar (detour from Omkareshwar)

Explore Ahilya Fort and famous Maheshwari saree weaving centers.

Mandu (2 hours)

Afghan-era architecture including Jahaz Mahal and other historic monuments.

Patalpani Waterfall (1 hour)

Scenic waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary (30 mins)

Nature trails and chance to spot various wildlife species.
